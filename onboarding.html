Welcome to Accessibility Helper

Your tool for providing feedback on website accessibility experiences

1

Getting Started

Accessibility Helper makes it easy to provide structured feedback about your experiences with website accessibility. Click the extension icon in your toolbar to access the feedback form.

This extension is designed to help collect valuable insights from users about their real-world experiences with website accessibility, which helps developers improve their websites for everyone, including people with disabilities.

2

Key Features

Our accessibility feedback tool offers:

Multi-page Feedback Form

A structured, step-by-step approach to collecting your experiences with website accessibility.

Accessibility Preferences

Document how you interact with websites and what challenges you commonly encounter.

Website-specific Feedback

Provide detailed feedback about specific websites you're evaluating.

Secure Data Submission

Your feedback is securely transmitted to our database to help improve web accessibility.

Export Functionality

Export your feedback data in JSON format for your records or further analysis.

3

Customize Your Experience

Click the settings icon (⚙️) in the extension popup to access customization options:

  • Choose between light, dark, or high contrast themes
  • Adjust font sizes for better readability
  • Configure sync settings
  • Set accessibility preferences

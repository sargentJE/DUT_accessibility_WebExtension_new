Accessibility

Keyboard Shortcuts Reference

Opening & Closing

  • Alt Shift A
    Open extension popup (global shortcut)
  • Escape
    Close popup or modal

Page Navigation

  • Ctrl ←
    Previous page
  • Ctrl →
    Next page
  • Ctrl 1 to 8
    Jump to specific page
  • Alt Home
    Scroll to top of current page

Quick Actions

  • Ctrl S
    Save current progress
  • Ctrl E
    Export review data
  • F1
    Open help documentation
  • Ctrl ,
    Open this settings panel
  • Ctrl Shift T
    Toggle theme

Form Navigation

  • Tab Shift Tab
    Navigate between form fields
  • Space
    Toggle checkboxes and activate buttons
  • Enter
    Activate buttons and submit forms
  • ↑ ↓
    Navigate radio buttons and select dropdowns

💡 Smart Context: Most shortcuts are disabled while typing in form fields to prevent interference with your input.

⚙️ Customization: You can customize the global shortcut by visiting chrome://extensions/shortcuts in your browser.

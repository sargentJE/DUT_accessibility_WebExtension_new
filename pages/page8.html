Thank You For Your Valuable Feedback!

Your expertise as a person with lived experience of disability is irreplaceable in improving web accessibility.

Your detailed feedback has been saved and will be shared with the website's development team. Your insights identify real barriers that automated testing tools simply cannot detect.

Why your perspective matters: Website developers often rely too heavily on automated accessibility checkers, which can miss up to 70% of actual barriers. Your firsthand experience reveals the real-world impact of design decisions.

Click Submit to finalize your responses. When you submit, your feedback will be securely sent to the development team. No personally identifying information is included.

What happens next:

  • The website owner receives a detailed accessibility report containing your feedback
  • Your specific user profile helps contextualize your experiences
  • Your prioritized suggestions guide developers on what to fix first
  • Your feedback is combined with others to identify patterns of barriers

Privacy note: Your personal information is kept confidential. Only your feedback and relevant accessibility needs are shared with the site owner.

After submission, you'll receive a confirmation that your feedback was successfully recorded. You can then close this extension.

Developers will review your specific barriers and prioritize fixes based on impact and frequency. Your contribution helps make the web more accessible for everyone.

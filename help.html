Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use this extension?

Simply click on the extension icon to open the popup, which will guide you through a series of questions about your accessibility experience with the current website. Fill out each page of the form and navigate through them using the buttons at the bottom.

What kind of feedback can I provide?

You can share your experiences about website accessibility, including how you interact with websites, what assistive technologies you use, challenges you encounter, and specific issues you've noticed on particular websites.

How do I submit my feedback?

Complete all pages of the form, and on the final page, click the "Submit Feedback" button. Your responses will be securely transmitted to our database for analysis.

How do I change settings?

Click the gear icon (⚙️) in the top right corner of any page to access settings. From there, you can customize various options like theme, font size, and feature preferences.

What happens to my feedback?

Your feedback helps web developers understand real-world accessibility challenges and improve their websites accordingly. All feedback is collected anonymously unless you specifically provide identifying information.

How can I get support?

For additional support, contact us at support@myvision.org.uk or visit our support page.

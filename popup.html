Why Your Insight Matters

  • Simple truth: Your real experiences matter more than any automated test.
  • Direct impact: Developers use your feedback to fix specific problems.
  • Valuable perspective: You'll point out issues others might never notice.
  • No technical knowledge needed: Just share what works and what doesn't.

What You'll Need

  • A public website open in another browser tab.
  • Any assistive tools you normally use (screen reader, voice control, etc.).
  • Time and freedom to note both positives and problems.

Privacy & Data Use

We store only the answers you type in this tool. Your data is encrypted and shared solely with the site owner to improve accessibility. No personal details are required.

Your feedback will be used to identify specific accessibility issues, prioritize fixes, and improve the experience for all users with similar needs.

How This Review Works

  1. Open a website you want to test in another tab.
